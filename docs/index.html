MathForces

"For the things we have to learn before we can do them, we learn by doing them."
- Aristotle, the Nichomachean Ethics

At MathForces, we believe that mastery is only achieved through challenge. We are devoted to providing a platform and database where students of mathematics can solve challenges and improve their skills. We also hope to plan a universal rating system to help people study specific problem sets at their personal skill level.
